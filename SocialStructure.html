Social Structure

On our social structure we have seperate levels of social status be we focus on skill and intelligence. We do this since we want to put people in jobs they deserve rather than being put there by an Emperor. If you also notice, we have craftspeople above the merchants. We do this because we value hard work rather than people making money off of other people's hard work. The way we put people into certain jobs is by making them take a test. The scores on the test will determine your job.
